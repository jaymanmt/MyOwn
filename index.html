ABOUT US

Have you come across news or information somewhere for e.g the internet, chatgroups? And it affected you in some way? It might have altered your perception on certain issues and have you start talking to others about it. Moments later, if it gathers enough attention, an expert in the industry (politician, scientist or economist) addresses it and it turns out to be mostly fake news. You have most likely been manipulated by someone/advertiser who would benefit in some way from your reaction whether is it in the form of your decision as a voter, consumer or even in the form of the likes on social media platforms. My intention with this webpage/blog is to STOP these fake assumptions and instill a sense of academia to anyone and everyone. Whether you have had an education or not, does not hinder your power as a consumer whose everyday decisions impact the world we live in and ultimately, the people around you. We need to accept that society revolves around the us, the consumers and if we can control our position as a collective, we can control society in doing better for the planet itself. In turn, the planet will reward us accordingly.
